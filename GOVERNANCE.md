## Project Governance

This document describes how the AtoZ Vehicle Collision Avoidance System project is governed.

The project is developed and maintained by **AtoZ Automation Solutions Pvt. Ltd.** with active participation from the open-source community.

---

## Goals

- Deliver a high-quality, trustworthy GPS-based collision avoidance platform.
- Encourage safe, privacy-conscious, and ethical use of real-time location data.
- Enable contributions from individuals, organizations, and research groups.

---

## Roles & Responsibilities

### 1. Project Owners (AtoZ Automation Solutions Pvt. Ltd.)

- Own the project’s **vision**, **roadmap**, and **brand/trademarks**.
- Appoint and remove maintainers.
- Make final decisions on:
  - Project direction and scope.
  - License changes and legal matters.
  - Security and responsible disclosure responses.

### 2. Maintainers

- Review and merge pull requests.
- Triage issues and categorize them.
- Ensure code quality, security, and style guidelines.
- Help facilitate community discussions and resolve conflicts.

Maintainers are typically long-term contributors or staff from AtoZ Automation Solutions Pvt. Ltd. or trusted partner organizations.

### 3. Contributors

- Submit issues, bug reports, and feature requests.
- Submit pull requests (code, documentation, tests).
- Participate in design discussions and reviews.

Contributors must follow:

- `CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md`
- `CONTRIBUTING.md`

---

## Decision Making

1. **Day-to-day decisions**  
   - Handled by maintainers via issue and PR review.

2. **Significant technical or architectural changes**  
   - Should be proposed in a GitHub issue or Request for Comments (RFC).
   - Discussed openly with the community.
   - Final decision made by Project Owners in consultation with maintainers.

3. **Licensing and legal changes**  
   - Determined by AtoZ Automation Solutions Pvt. Ltd. with input from legal counsel.

4. **Security issues**  
   - Coordinated privately following `SECURITY.md`.
   - Fixes are released and communicated according to an agreed disclosure timeline.

---

## Release Management

- Releases follow **semantic versioning** where possible:
  - `MAJOR.MINOR.PATCH`
- Release notes are published in:
  - `CHANGELOG.md`
  - GitHub Releases (if used)
- Security or critical bug fixes may result in out-of-cycle patch releases.

---

## Trademarks & Branding

- The names, logos, and visual identity associated with:
  - **AtoZ Automation Solutions Pvt. Ltd.**
  - The official name of this collision avoidance system
  are trademarks or trade names of AtoZ Automation Solutions Pvt. Ltd.

- Community members may:
  - Refer to the project in descriptive ways (“based on the AtoZ Vehicle Collision Avoidance System”).
  - Use the project name in compliance with any published trademark guidelines.

- Community members may **not**:
  - Present forks or derivatives as official AtoZ products.
  - Use the AtoZ logo or brand in a way that suggests endorsement without permission.

A separate Trademark Policy may be published and updated over time.

---

## Amendments

This governance document may be updated by AtoZ Automation Solutions Pvt. Ltd. as the project evolves. Significant changes will be:

- Committed to this repository.
- Optionally announced via release notes, project blog, or community channels.
